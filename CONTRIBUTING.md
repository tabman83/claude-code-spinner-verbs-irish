# Contributing

Got a verb the list is missing? Grand, send it on.

## How

1. Fork the repo.
2. Add your verb to `spinner-verbs-dublin.json`, keeping the array alphabetical-ish or thematic-ish — whatever feels right.
3. Open a PR.

## What makes a good verb

- **Generic.** It should describe a state, an effort, or a mood — not a specific task. *"Wreckin' me head"* works. *"Compilin' the code"* doesn't.
- **Authentic.** Something a Dub would actually say, not something a tourist thinks they'd say.
- **Short-ish.** Long enough to be funny, short enough to fit in a terminal.
- **No duplicates.** Have a quick scan first.

## What won't make it in

- Anything mean-spirited about a person, place, or community.
- Anything that pretends to be doing a real coding task.
- *"Top o' the mornin'."* Don't.
